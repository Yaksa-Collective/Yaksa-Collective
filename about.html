About

Yaksa is becoming a home for the work itself.

Rather than acting as a placeholder for a wider ecosystem, the site now centres poems, prose, and practice. The aim is simplicity, truthfulness, and a structure that can grow naturally over time.

What Yaksa is for

A quiet digital house for poems, essays, and contemplative writing. A place where food, mind, labour, and Buddhist reflection can meet without pretending to be louder or larger than they are.

How it can develop

The structure is intentionally minimal: Home, Poems, Writings, Practice, About, Contact. That gives the site room to breathe now and room to deepen later.
